Movement Notes

VEIL patterns never open with a drop. They slip across a single adjacent column, as if trying not to be noticed.

SHIFT events collapse vertically. When technicians annotate a stable drop, they mark it twice, stacked.

HOME returns are corrective rather than direct: retrace once, recover height once, then settle one column farther toward origin.

LATCH demonstrations were retained for public footage even after review. Operators complained that the sequence looked cleaner than the real handling pattern.
